Hi, I ’m Erika.

I believe in design driven by purpose. I am a freelance web & print designer who aims to help businesses, individuals, and organizations make a positive impact. Please, check out some of my work, read more about me, and if you have a project for me, let’s get in touch!

My Work.

About Me.

I started out my design career by working for competitive interactive agencies in Los Angeles. Over the years, I’ve become increasingly passionate about environmental and social issues, and my design philosophy has evolved to reflect that shift. For me, design is much more than creating something aesthetically pleasing and easy to use. When driven by a higher purpose, design can be an incredible force for positive change.

If you are a non-profit, an environmentally or socially friendly business, a startup, or other type of do-good organization, I want to work with you, so let’s chat!

When I’m not creating beautiful web sites for my clients, I can be found practicing yoga, hula hooping, dancing, reading and being in nature.

I’m also an avid traveler, and these days with wifi available in even the most obscure locations, I can take my work with me wherever I go. I love photography, and all the pictures on this site are from my travels.
